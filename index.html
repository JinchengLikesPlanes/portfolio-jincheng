About Me

Hello! My name is Jincheng and I am currently a first year student at the University of Toronto in the Engineering Science program. My dream is to work in the aerospace industry, something I have been deeply passionate about for a long while.

Throuhgout high school, I dedicated myself into understanding mathematics and physics. I have also won multple awards as well as becoming #1 in my school in various competitions including the AMC 12, AIME, Euclid, and many more.

Outside of STEM, I also have many different hobbies such as sketching, blender modelling, and figure skating. But most importantly, I love to learn and I strive to become the best version of myself.

I created this website to show you a deep dive into who I am as a person. Over here, I will update on complete projects as well as creating blogs that show progressions I make. My goal is to share my mistakes, procceses, and findings with everyone so that we can all learn together.
